About Crypto Tracker

Crypto Tracker is a modern Flask web application that displays real-time cryptocurrency prices, market trends, and market capitalization using the CoinGecko API. It offers a clean dashboard, fast search, responsive design, and live market updates for popular cryptocurrencies.
